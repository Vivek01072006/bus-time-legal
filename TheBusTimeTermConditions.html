1. About the Application

The Bus Time is designed to help schools, drivers, and parents manage and monitor school transportation activities, including:

Live bus tracking and real-time location updates
Student management and attendance monitoring
Communication features between schools, drivers, and parents
Route planning and management
Emergency alerts and notifications
Note: The Bus Time is developed and maintained by CVaint Technologies.

2. User Categories

The application is used by the following types of users:

School Admin
Manages school profile and settings
Adds/removes drivers and parents
Monitors all bus activities
Handles subscription management
Driver
Updates trip status (Start/End)
Shares live location during duty
Follows assigned routes
Communicates with school/parents
Parent
Tracks child's bus in real-time
Receives notifications and alerts
Views driver and route information
Manages child's pickup details

Each user role has specific permissions and responsibilities within the app.
